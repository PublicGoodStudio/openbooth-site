Measured benefits

Open working environments overexpose us. Too many sounds. Too many distractions. They force us to multi-task and keep us from being our best selves.

When we increase our privacy, our focus improves, and we are able to perform at significantly higher levels. We become more creative, our cognitive abilities sharpen, our stress levels decrease,

our sick days drop, and we add 15% to the bottom line.

The benefits of working in a focused space are clear and long established. And, thanks to the lightweight nature of the spaces, you can pop them up in an afternoon - instantly adding an environment that encourages focus, happiness, and productivity.

A product of libraries

OpenBooth started in the Harvard Library Innovation Lab as an effort to provide lightweight spaces for phone conversations.

During our research, we came to understand that many people, primarily those working in open offices, lack a secure space to speak on the phone.

We founded Public Good Studio to continue our reserach,

and offer OpenBooths to everyone for a fair price.
